**Selected benchmark:** Revenue (most stable benchmark for a manufacturing entity with volatile margins).

**Overall materiality:** PKR 5,000,000

**Performance materiality (75% of overall):** PKR 3,750,000

**Trivial threshold (5% of overall):** PKR 250,000

### Justification

Revenue is selected as the benchmark because: (a) it is the most stable metric over the three-year trend; (b) the primary users (shareholders, banks) focus on revenue-driven covenants; (c) profit before tax is volatile due to FX movements and would produce an unstable materiality level.

## 5. Audit Strategy Summary

### Approach

| Area               | Strategy                                          | Rationale                                                                   |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Revenue — export   | Substantive (test of details)                     | Verify shipment documents, LC, and bank receipts for sample of transactions |
| Revenue — domestic | Combined (controls + substantive)                 | Test invoicing controls; substantive cut-off testing                        |
| Inventory          | Substantive (physical attendance + valuation)     | Attend year-end count at Faisalabad factory; test NRV for finished goods    |
| Trade receivables  | Substantive (confirmations + subsequent receipts) | Circularise top 15 debtors; test ECL model assumptions                      |
| PPE                | Substantive (additions/disposals only)            | Verify additions > PKR 1M to invoice and physical inspection                |
| Bank borrowings    | Substantive (bank confirmations)                  | Direct confirmation with HBL and MCB                                        |
| Related parties    | Substantive (detail testing)                      | Verify intercompany balances and pricing against agreements                 |
| Tax                | Specialist involvement                            | Engage tax team for deferred tax review and open assessment risk            |
