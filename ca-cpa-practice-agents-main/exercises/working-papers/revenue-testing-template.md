# Revenue Testing Working Paper

> This is fictional data for educational purposes only.
> Template for substantive testing of revenue — Crescent Textiles (Pvt) Ltd.

---

## 1. Objective

To obtain sufficient appropriate audit evidence that revenue is:

- **Occurring** (recorded sales represent genuine transactions)
- **Complete** (all sales are recorded)
- **Accurately stated** (amounts agree to source documents)
- **Recorded in the correct period** (cut-off is appropriate)
- **Properly classified** (export vs. domestic, gross vs. net)
