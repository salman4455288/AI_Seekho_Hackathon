## Key Risks

| Risk                     | Description                                                                                                                                                           |
| ------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Input cost inflation     | Wheat flour, sugar, and edible oil constitute ~60% of COGS. Prices subject to government regulation and global commodity shifts.                                      |
| Regulatory (food safety) | Subject to Pakistan Standards and Quality Control Authority (PSQCA) and Punjab Food Authority inspections. Non-compliance risk on labelling and shelf-life standards. |
| Distribution             | Relies on third-party distributors for ~70% of domestic sales. Loss of a major distributor would impact revenue for 2-3 months during transition.                     |
| Seasonality              | Revenue peaks in Q2 (Oct-Dec, Eid and winter season) and dips in Q4 (Apr-Jun, summer). Working capital needs fluctuate accordingly.                                   |
| Parent dependency        | Financial and operational support from Crescent Group. Standalone viability depends on continued access to group facilities and financing.                            |
